Design Rationale:

Why remove sparkles? User feedback: "you definitely can't tell what the sparkles are on the page. You just see a blip of yellow." Sparkles became unrecognizable "yellow blobs" at 16√ó16px and 40√ó40px.

Why no lines in favicon? At 16√ó16px, the 3 document lines would become unreadable noise. Extreme simplification needed for clarity.

Why thicker strokes in favicon? Smaller display size requires bolder strokes for visibility. 14px strokes ensure curly braces are recognizable even at tiny sizes.
