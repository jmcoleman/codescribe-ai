💡 Design Recommendation

For a portfolio project showcasing to technical recruiters and hiring managers, I recommend Option 2: "CS" Monogram.

Here's why:

Decision Framework

Option 1: Document + Sparkle Best for: Consumer apps, broad audiences, emphasizing "what it does" over brand. Safe but generic.
Option 2: "CS" Monogram ⭐ RECOMMENDED Best for: Professional portfolio, B2B SaaS, establishing brand identity. Shows design maturity. Like Notion, Asana, Linear.
Option 3: Abstract Geometric Best for: Creative studios, design-forward companies, when you have a full branding team. More risky but distinctive.

Why "CS" monogram wins for your portfolio:
• Demonstrates brand thinking (not just "AI tool #47")
• Professional and polished (like successful SaaS companies)
• Memorable for recruiters ("that's the CodeScribe app")
• Works perfectly at all sizes (especially 16×16 browser tabs)
• Small AI sparkle accent maintains the tech/AI connection
• Shows restraint and design maturity

The document icon is descriptive but not distinctive. The monogram builds brand equity while the small sparkle keeps the AI context clear.
